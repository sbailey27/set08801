NOW AT LONG LAST, THE RECIPE!!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb cheddar cheese
  • 1 lb minced pork (or any meat of your choice)
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • 1 pack oven ready lasagna noodles (cause who has time to boil nooodles huh??)

    • Directions

    Follow these EXACTLY or I'm calling the police:

  • Skillet. High heat. Lil bit of oil. In goes the meat. Sear that meat like it insulted your mother.
  • Scorch that meat till it's cooked thoroughly. Then toss in the salt and spices to taste.
  • Reduce heat and empty that jar of pasta sauce into the meat. Mix it all around and let it cook for a few minutes to thicken up.
  • When that's done, IT"S LAYERING TIME!!!
  • LISTEN CLOSELY: meat sauce. sheet. cheese. repeat. Got it? meat sauce. sheet, cheese repeat.
  • Bake for 40 minutes covered in foil. Remove foil for the last 10 minutes of baking for that cheesy crust
